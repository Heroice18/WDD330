The Master Mobile UX Video

  • I like the part where it talks about designing for the smallest and least powerful device first. In today's market there are so many different types and styles of smartphones, that if you want your product to succeed in the market you need to make sure that the product is able to reach as many people as possible.
  • It's interesting to see the exponential growth of smartphones over the years. I'd be interested to know the different models that helped the growth over the past 10 years or so.
  • I like how the video states that if we design for mobile first then we're making it easier to iterate upon the platform in future updates.
  • "Start small and scale up."
