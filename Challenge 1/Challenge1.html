INFO Welcome to the TODO task manager!
Here you can view and track your tasks from anywhere you want!

To mark a task complete toggle the button on the left of the text
To remove a task click the button on the right of the task
You can also see how many tasks you have yet to complete and filter which tasks are showing using the filter bar.
To add a task type in a title for the filter and click on the + button

Have fun and be productive!
